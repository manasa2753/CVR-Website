Our Courses

Courses We Offer

CVR College of Engineering offers B.Tech. courses in Civil, CSBS, CSE, CSE (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning), CSE (Cyber Security), CSE (Data Science), ECE, EEE, EE (VLSI), EIE, IT and Mechanical Engineering.

B.Tech

Admission to the B.Tech programs at CVR College of Engineering typically involves a combination of Class 12 merit and entrance exam scores, such as the TS EAMCET.The duration of the B.Tech programs is 4 years.

M.Tech

CVR offers M.E./M.Tech programme to the students. This 2 years course is offered in Full Time mode. All candidates interested in seeking M.E./M.Tech admission at CVR College of Engineering need to possess a valid GATE and TS PGECET score.

Our facilities

CVR College of Engineering offers a range of facilities including spacious classrooms with LCD projectors, dedicated labs, an independent library, a student center with a canteen, gym, and indoor sports, an auditorium, and hostel facilities.

Library

The CVR college of Engineering library is a dedicated three-story building with a total area of 1450 sqm, designed for library and postgraduate research.

Largest Playground

The CVR College playground is a vibrant hub for athletic activity and camaraderie, offering a spacious area for various sports and games.

Tasty and Healthy Food

The CVR College cafeteria is a vibrant hub for students, offering a delicious and affordable array of food options.
