About Us

We are the largest university

CVR College of Engineering, established in 2001 and located in Hyderabad, Telangana, is a premier private engineering institution known for its commitment to academic excellence and innovation. Affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad and approved by AICTE, the college offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology. It is accredited by the NBA and NAAC with an 'A' grade and recognized as an autonomous institution by the UGC. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, dedicated faculty, and a focus on research and holistic development, CVR College provides a dynamic learning environment that nurtures future-ready engineers and leaders.
